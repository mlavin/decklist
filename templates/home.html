How It Works

Create a Deck

Export your favorite deck from Pokémon Trading Card Game Online (PTCGO) or copy one of the tournament winning lists from the Pokémon Championship Series Events.

Upload the Deck

Take your deck list exported in the equivalent PTCGO list format and paste it into our uploader form. We parse the list and find the price of all the cards for you.

Adjust the Cards

Before purchasing the cards you can adjust the numbers to account for cards you already own. With those adjustments you can see the cost of only the cards you need to complete the deck.
